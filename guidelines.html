Logo Misuse

To maintain brand integrity, it's crucial to use our logo correctly. Please avoid the following common misuses.

Don't Stretch or Distort

Always scale the logo proportionally. Do not stretch or compress it in any way.

Don't Change Colors

Only use the official brand colors. Do not recolor the logo.

Don't Add Effects

Do not add drop shadows, glows, gradients, or other visual effects to the logo.

Don't Use on Low-Contrast Backgrounds

Ensure there is sufficient contrast between the logo and the background for readability.
